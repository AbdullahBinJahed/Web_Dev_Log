Some nice yuri mangas

  • シトラス (Japanese); Citrus (Saburo Uta); A yuri manga between two step-sisters.
  • 一度だけでも、後悔してます (Japanese); Even If It Was Just Once, I Regret It; A romance comedy that seemingly starts with sex.
  • やがて君になる (Japanese); Bloom Into You; A yuri manga between two high school girls who figures out their sexuality.
